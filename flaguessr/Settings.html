What is Flaguessr? 🌎

Flaguessr is an online interactive game designed to help you learn the flags of the world. Currently, it offers four game modes: Flashcards & Maps to study the flags; and Quizzes & Map Quizzes to put into practice your knowledge. The flags are organized by continents. However, there is also the option to choose all continents at once or to learn the flags of U.S. States.

How do I play? 🎮

- Flashcards: Use the 'Right' and 'Left' Arrow keys to move through cards. Press 'Space' to flip the cards. When the card is flipped, you can press 'w' to open the Wikipedia page of the respective flag, or 'm' to locate it on Google Maps. There is also the option to restart the flashcards by pressing 'r', and exit with 'e'. In case of using mouse or touchscreen, use the buttons to do the same.

- Quiz: Use the input box to enter your guess. Press 'Enter' or click on the arrow to submit the selected country. As only valid inputs are allowed, a suggestion box will pop up under to help you. Move through the suggestions with the 'Up' and 'Down' Arrow keys. Note that inputs in lower and upper case are both valid. Foreign characters (not from the latin alphabet) aren't used on the quiz, so use the most similar letter instead. Example: 'c' instead of 'ç'. In such cases, we recommend using the suggestion box as a guide. Each quiz has as many questions as the respective continent has countries, except for the "All continents" quiz, which has only 30. The "U.S. States" quizzes you on all 50 states.

- Map: Click or hover on the desired map region to find out its name and flag.

- Map Quiz: Flags of the chosen continent will be displayed on the bottom of the screen. For each flag, you will have one try to click on the corresponding territory on the map. If it's correct, the country you selected will become green. Else, the correct country will become red.

Sources 📚

The images and flags used in this website are owned by Flagpedia.net. Flagpedia is an actively maintained and updated website with a goal to provide the best overview and source of flags of the world. Flag images from this website are over 14 years widely used in news magazines, websites, software, mobile apps and master's thesis. You can find hundreds of downloadable flags, in different sizes and formats. Check out their website at:

www.flagpedia.net

Note that its implementation might slow down the games when playing for the first time. Once all flags are loaded, the program runs well! ⚠️
